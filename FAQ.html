Frequently Asked Questions

Are there family-friendly accommodations and activities? - Yes, the island has many family-friendly resorts and hotels, as well as activities suitable for all ages, such as beach outings, nature walks, and interactive cultural experiences.

What is the best time of year to visit? - The island enjoys a tropical climate year-round, but the best time to visit is during the dry season, from November to April, when the weather is sunny and pleasant.

What should I pack for my trip? - Pack light, breathable clothing, swimwear, sunscreen, a hat, and comfortable walking shoes. Don’t forget your camera to capture the beautiful scenery and memorable moments!

Do I need a visa to visit the island? - Visa requirements vary depending on your country of origin. It’s best to check with the local embassy or consulate for the most up-to-date information.
