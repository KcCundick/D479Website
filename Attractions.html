Attractions on the Island of Taniti

Taniti Botanical Gardens: A beautifully landscaped garden showcasing the diverse flora of Taniti, including rare tropical plants and a butterfly conservatory.

Turtle Sanctuary and Rehab Center: A sanctuary dedicated to the conservation and rehabilitation of sea turtles, where visitors can learn about these majestic creatures and even participate in releasing rehabilitated turtles back into the ocean.

Sunset Beach Cinema: An open-air cinema on the beach where visitors can watch movies under the stars while enjoying the sound of the waves.
